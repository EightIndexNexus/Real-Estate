Who We Are

A Category of One

EightIndex Inc does not build apartments. We build ecosystems where residents live, work, and grow their businesses without ever leaving the property.

We identified a category gap that no developer has filled at scale: the remote professional and entrepreneur who needs a registered business address, boardroom access, and corporate infrastructure — but refuses to pay separately for WeWork on top of rent.

The Nexus is our answer. A Class-A residential community where every unit includes a dedicated home office den, every resident receives a professional registered business address, and the Nexus Clubhouse delivers full corporate-grade infrastructure — all included in rent.
